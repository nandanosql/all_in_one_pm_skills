## Purpose

Identify and categorize stakeholders by **influence, interest, and alignment** — then design tailored communication to keep the right people informed, engaged, or out of the way.

---

## Application

### Stakeholder Grid

```
              HIGH INFLUENCE
              │
    Manage    │    Partner
    Closely   │    Closely
              │
LOW ──────────┼────────── HIGH
INTEREST      │           INTEREST
              │
    Monitor   │    Keep
    Only      │    Informed
              │
              LOW INFLUENCE
```

### Template

```markdown
# Stakeholder Map: [Initiative Name]

| Stakeholder | Role | Influence | Interest | Alignment | Strategy |
|------------|------|-----------|----------|-----------|---------|
| [Name] | [Title] | H/M/L | H/M/L | 👍/😐/👎 | [Partner/Inform/Manage/Monitor] |
| [Name] | [Title] | H/M/L | H/M/L | 👍/😐/👎 | [Strategy] |

## Communication Plan

### Partner Closely (High Influence + High Interest)
- **Who:** [Names]
- **Cadence:** [Weekly 1:1 or biweekly sync]
- **Channel:** [Meetings + Slack]
- **What they need:** [Detailed progress, early input on decisions]

### Keep Informed (Low Influence + High Interest)
- **Who:** [Names]
- **Cadence:** [Biweekly email update]
- **What they need:** [Progress updates, outcomes]

### Manage Closely (High Influence + Low Interest)
- **Who:** [Names]
- **Cadence:** [Monthly summary]
- **What they need:** [High-level outcomes, risks only]

### Monitor (Low Influence + Low Interest)
- **Who:** [Names]
- **Cadence:** [Quarterly or as needed]
- **What they need:** [Only if directly impacted]
```
