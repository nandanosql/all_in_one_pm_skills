## Purpose

Write concise async stakeholder updates that communicate **progress, blockers, risks, and asks** — enabling stakeholders to stay informed without attending meetings.

---

## Application

### Stakeholder Update Template

```markdown
# [Project Name] — Update [Date]

**Overall Health:** 🟢 On Track / 🟡 At Risk / 🔴 Blocked
**Sprint/Period:** [Current sprint or date range]

## ✅ Completed This Period
- [Accomplishment 1 — in business terms, not technical]
- [Accomplishment 2]
- [Accomplishment 3]

## 🔄 In Progress
- [Work item 1] — [% complete or status]
- [Work item 2] — [Status]

## 🚧 Blockers & Risks
- **[Blocker 1]:** [Description + what's needed to unblock]
- **[Risk 1]:** [Description + mitigation in progress]

## 📊 Key Metrics
| Metric | Target | Actual | Status |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| [Metric 1] | [Target] | [Current] | 🟢/🟡/🔴 |

## 🎯 Next Period Goals
- [Goal 1 — what we'll accomplish]
- [Goal 2]

## ❓ Asks / Decisions Needed
- [Ask 1 — specific request + from whom]
- [Decision 1 — what needs deciding + deadline]
```

### Writing Rules

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| Lead with health status | Stakeholders want the headline first |
| Use business language | "Improved trial conversion" not "Deployed OAuth provider" |
| Be explicit about asks | Don't hint — state what you need from whom |
| Keep under 1 page | Respect their time; link to details |
| Send consistently | Same day, same time, every period |

---

## Quality Rubric

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Clarity** | Jargon-heavy | Mostly clear | Business-language, scannable |
| **Completeness** | Progress only | Progress + risks | Progress + risks + metrics + asks |
| **Consistency** | Ad hoc timing | Regular but informal | Same format, same cadence, every period |
| **Actionability** | FYI only | Some asks | Explicit asks with owners + deadlines |
