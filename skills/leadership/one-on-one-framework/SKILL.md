## Purpose

Structure effective 1:1 meetings that **build trust, surface problems early, and accelerate career growth** — not status updates that could be done async.

---

## Application

### 1:1 Formats by Relationship

#### 1:1 With Your Direct Report

```markdown
## 1:1 Agenda: [Name] — [Date]
**Duration:** 30 min | **Cadence:** Weekly

### Their Topics (they share first — 15 min)
- [Topic 1]
- [Topic 2]

### Your Topics (10 min)
- [Feedback or coaching point]
- [Context they need]
- [Alignment on priorities]

### Standing Check-ins (5 min, rotate weekly)
- Week 1: Career growth — "How's your development going?"
- Week 2: Team health — "How's the team dynamic?"
- Week 3: Personal — "How are you doing overall?"
- Week 4: Feedback — "What can I do better as your manager?"
```

#### 1:1 With Your Manager

```markdown
## 1:1 Prep: [Manager Name] — [Date]

### Updates (2 min — do this async if possible)
- [Key win this week]
- [Key risk/blocker]

### Discussion Topics (25 min)
- [Decision I need input on]
- [Context I need from you]
- [Coaching request]

### Ask for Feedback (3 min)
- "Is there anything I should be doing differently?"
```

#### 1:1 With Cross-Functional Peer

```markdown
## 1:1 Agenda: [Peer Name] — [Date]
**Duration:** 30 min | **Cadence:** Biweekly / Monthly

- [Shared initiative status]
- [Upcoming dependencies]
- [How we're working together — any friction?]
- [How can I help you?]
```

### 1:1 Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Fix |
|-------------|-----|
| Status report meeting | Do status async; use 1:1 for coaching/growth |
| PM talks 80% of the time | Let the other person share first |
| Skip when busy | Never skip — reschedule if needed |
| No follow-up on commitments | Track action items, follow up |
| All business, no human connection | Start with genuine check-in |

---

## Quality Rubric

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Preparation** | Wing it | Some topics prepared | Both parties prep topics |
| **Balance** | One person talks 80%+ | Somewhat balanced | 50/50 conversation |
| **Growth** | Status only | Some coaching | Regular career discussion |
| **Follow-through** | Commitments forgotten | Some tracking | All commitments followed up |
