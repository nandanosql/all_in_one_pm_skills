## Purpose

Help PMs take control of their time — which is their **scarcest resource** — by auditing calendar usage, protecting focus time, managing energy, and learning to say no strategically.

---

## Application

### Step 1: Calendar Audit

```markdown
## Current Time Allocation

Track one week and categorize every hour:

| Category | Hours/Week | % | Ideal % |
|----------|-----------|---|---------|
| Strategic work (research, planning, writing) | [#] | [%] | 25-30% |
| Talking to customers/users | [#] | [%] | 15-20% |
| Team meetings (standups, planning, retros) | [#] | [%] | 15-20% |
| Stakeholder meetings | [#] | [%] | 10-15% |
| Email/Slack/admin | [#] | [%] | 10-15% |
| Reactive (urgent requests, fires) | [#] | [%] | 5-10% |
| **Total** | 40-50 | 100% | — |

**Gap:** [Where is worst-vs-ideal mismatch?]
```

### Step 2: Apply PM Time Management Rules

```markdown
1. **Protect 2-3 blocks of 2+ hour focus time weekly**
   - Calendar block them; treat as non-negotiable
   - Use for PRDs, research synthesis, strategic thinking

2. **Batch similar work**
   - All 1:1s on one day
   - All stakeholder meetings on another
   - User calls in dedicated slots

3. **Apply the "Does this need me?" test**
   - Before accepting: "Will this meeting fail without me?"
   - If no → decline or send a delegate

4. **Energy management > time management**
   - Strategic work during peak energy (morning for most)
   - Meetings during low-energy periods
   - Never schedule creative work after back-to-back meetings
```

### Step 3: Saying No Framework

```markdown
| Request Type | Response |
|-------------|----------|
| "Can you join this meeting?" | "What decision needs my input? Can I async?" |
| "Can you add this to the roadmap?" | "Let's evaluate using our prioritization framework" |
| "Can you take on project X?" | "Yes, if we deprioritize Y. Which should I drop?" |
| "Can we pick your brain?" | "Send me context first, I'll respond async or schedule 15 min" |
```

## Quality Rubric

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | No tracking | Annual reflection | Weekly calendar audit |
| **Focus Time** | 0 protected blocks | Some blocks (often sacrificed) | 2-3 protected blocks (defended) |
| **Saying No** | Accept everything | Occasionally decline | Strategic "no" with alternative |
