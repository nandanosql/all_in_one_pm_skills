## Purpose

Understand PM career progression and **assess current level** across four competency dimensions — Product Sense, Execution, Leadership, and Strategic Impact — enabling targeted development.

---

## Application

### PM Career Ladder

| Level | Title | Scope | Key Shift |
|-------|-------|-------|-----------|
| **L1** | Associate PM | Feature | "Ship features that work" |
| **L2** | Product Manager | Feature set | "Define what to build and why" |
| **L3** | Senior PM | Product area | "Own outcomes, not outputs" |
| **L4** | Group PM / Director | Product line | "Scale through others" |
| **L5** | VP Product | Portfolio | "Set strategy, build org" |
| **L6** | CPO | Company | "Business through product" |

### Competency Matrix

```markdown
## Self-Assessment: [Your Name]

| Competency | L1 | L2 | L3 | L4+ |
|-----------|----|----|----|----|
| **Product Sense** | Follow frameworks | Apply judgment | Set product direction | Set company direction |
| **Execution** | Ship assigned work | Drive cross-functional delivery | Remove systemic blockers | Build execution culture |
| **Leadership** | Contribute to team | Lead feature team | Lead PMs, mentor | Build + scale PM org |
| **Strategic Impact** | Feature-level metrics | Area-level outcomes | Portfolio strategy | Business model evolution |

**My current level:** [Self-assessment]
**Where I want to be:** [Target level]
**Gap:** [Key competencies to develop]
```
