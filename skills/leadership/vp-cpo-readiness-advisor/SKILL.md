## Purpose

Assess readiness for **VP/CPO-level product leadership** — evaluating organizational design capability, board-level communication, business model ownership, executive presence, and strategic leadership.

---

## Application

### Turn 1: Current Reality

**Agent asks:** "Where are you now?"
- Current title and scope
- Organization size (total product team)
- Reporting structure
- Board exposure
- P&L responsibility

### Turn 2: Executive Competency Assessment

```markdown
## VP/CPO Competency Assessment

### 1. Organizational Design
- [ ] Has built and structured a PM organization
- [ ] Hired and developed Director-level PMs
- [ ] Created product team operating model
- [ ] Managed org through scaling (10→50+ people)

### 2. Board & Investor Communication
- [ ] Presented to board of directors
- [ ] Answers investor product questions with confidence
- [ ] Communicates multi-year product vision and strategy
- [ ] Translates product into business narrative

### 3. Business Model Ownership
- [ ] Owns or co-owns P&L
- [ ] Makes pricing/monetization strategic decisions
- [ ] Understands unit economics deeply
- [ ] Drives strategic partnerships

### 4. Executive Presence
- [ ] Commands respect in the C-suite
- [ ] Navigates CEO relationship effectively
- [ ] Handles company-level crises with composure
- [ ] Represents product externally (press, conferences, customers)

### 5. Market & Technology Vision
- [ ] Anticipates market shifts 2-5 years out
- [ ] Makes technology bets (AI, platform, ecosystem)
- [ ] Shapes industry narrative
- [ ] Balances innovation with execution discipline
```
