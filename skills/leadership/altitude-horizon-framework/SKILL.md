## Purpose

Navigate between **strategic (30,000 ft) and tactical (ground level) thinking** — knowing when to zoom in for execution detail and when to zoom out for strategic perspective, and communicating at the right altitude for each audience.

---

## Application

### Altitude Levels

| Altitude | View | Thinking | Audience | Time Horizon |
|----------|------|---------|----------|-------------|
| **30,000 ft** | Vision & strategy | "Where are we going?" | Board, C-suite | 2-5 years |
| **20,000 ft** | Portfolio & priorities | "What should we bet on?" | VPs, directors | 1-2 years |
| **10,000 ft** | Roadmap & themes | "What are we building this quarter?" | Product team, stakeholders | 1-4 quarters |
| **5,000 ft** | Epics & features | "How does this feature work?" | Cross-functional team | 1-3 months |
| **Ground level** | Stories & tasks | "What do I do today?" | Individual contributors | 1-2 weeks |

### Self-Assessment

```markdown
## Altitude Check: [Your Name]

### Where I spend most time: [Altitude level]
### Where I should spend more time: [Altitude level]
### Why the gap: [Too tactical? Too strategic?]

### By Situation
| Situation | Appropriate Altitude | Where I Go | Adjustment |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------|-----------|
| Board meeting | 30,000 ft | [Where I go] | [Zoom out/stay/zoom in] |
| Sprint planning | Ground level | [Where I go] | [Stay/zoom out a bit] |
| 1:1 with eng lead | 5,000 ft | [Where I go] | [Adjustment] |
| Quarterly planning | 10,000 ft | [Where I go] | [Adjustment] |
| Customer discovery | 5-10,000 ft | [Where I go] | [Adjustment] |
```

### Communication Rules by Altitude

| Your Audience | Their Altitude | Your Message Should Include | Should NOT Include |
|--------------|---------------|---------------------------|-------------------|
| Board/CEO | 30,000 ft | Market trends, strategic bets | Sprint velocity |
| VP/Director | 20,000 ft | Portfolio tradeoffs, quarterly outcomes | Individual ticket details |
| Product Team | 10,000 ft | Roadmap themes, prioritization | Revenue projections |
| Engineers | Ground level | Requirements, acceptance criteria | Market sizing |

---

## Quality Rubric

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Stuck at one altitude | Recognizes 2-3 altitudes | Navigates all 5 fluidly |
| **Communication** | Same pitch for everyone | Some audience adaptation | Right altitude for right audience |
| **Transition** | Can't zoom in/out | Awkward transitions | Smooth, context-appropriate shifts |
