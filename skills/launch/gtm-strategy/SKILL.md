## Purpose

Build a GTM strategy that connects **product capabilities to market execution** — covering target segments, messaging, channels, pricing, sales motion, and launch phases. Ensures you don't just build something great but actually get it into the right hands.

---

## Application

### GTM Strategy Template

```markdown
# Go-to-Market Strategy: [Product/Feature Name]
**Owner:** [PM / Marketing Lead]
**Launch Date:** [Date]
**Market:** [Existing / New / Expansion]

---

## 1. Target Segments (in priority order)

### Beachhead Segment (Primary)
- **Who:** [Specific ICP]
- **Size:** [TAM/SAM/SOM]
- **Why them first:** [Why this segment is most accessible]

### Expansion Segment 1
- **Who:** [Next segment]
- **Unlock criteria:** [What needs to be true before going here]
```

## Quality Rubric

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Segments** | "Everyone" | Named segment | Prioritized segments with sizing |
| **Messaging** | Feature-focused | Benefit-focused | Segment-specific with proof |
| **Channels** | "We'll do marketing" | Channels listed | Channels + investment + expected CAC |
| **Phasing** | Big-bang launch | Phases listed | Phases with success criteria |
| **Metrics** | None | Metrics listed | Targets at 30/90 days |
